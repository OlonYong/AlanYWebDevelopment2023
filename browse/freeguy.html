Free Guy

Image Source: Vague Visages

15+

2021

My review

This is one of the BEST video games movies EVER made. This film easily exceeded all my expectations. This film was fresh, funny, unique, and clever. The special effects ARE AMAZING. One of the best special effects I’ve seen in a movie in AWHILE.

In my opinion the effects are slightly better than Ready Player Ones effects. Which really helps elevate this film and make it all the more entertaining to watch. I highly recommend you see it in 3D because it really helps to bring the world the writers and directors set up even more realistic and enthralling.

All the characters are fully fleshed out they are NOT just disposable action characters you see in FAR too many video game movies. They are acting is amazing Ryan Renolds REALLY made this film. This film wouldn’t have been as funny or emotionally investing without him. He also really fits the build aesthetic wise. Which made the film made the film more real. And surprisingly Joe Keery gave the best performance in the entire film.

This film was filled with lovable characters. And a really intriguing story line. The ending was also really heartwarming. This is DEFINITELY a feel-good movie.

The action scenes were all MEMORABLE and well-choreographed. Ryan Renolds and Jodie Comer did a great job in ALL those scenes. I will definitely remember them.

That being said this isn’t a perfect film. Some of the jokes didn’t land well. Taika Waititi was miscast for his role and overacted quite a bit. His slap stick performance didn’t fit well with tone of the rest of the movie. The movie also sometimes gets pretty clique at times where it just didn’t need to be.

But the positives DRASTICALLY outweigh the negatives. It’s definitely worth your time and money. Go see this film there really isn’t another one quite like it. It’s worth the price for admission simply due to the special effects and elaborate and memorable action sequences.
