Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

Image Source: Merchoid

15+

2023

My review

A film with a great length, that tries not to skip through every moment. The montages are carefully chosen, and it shows that. It has its charm, and comedic moments that the series paved. Great, original comedy too, and some tired jokes that needed rewriting...

But for what is supposed to be the final film, cut out a main character for 50% of the film, and left you already eager for another one to compensate. A typical got to save a friend storyline, which was elaborated on well, and cleverly twisted around for fun, in a great way. But That ends with this friendship, they spend the entire film expressing, very openly, to break up like a band.

It has a lot of that sort of confusing, twisting and turning emotionally bipolar, pseudo emo personality around it. Idk how else to explain.

I found the ending attempted an unnatural feel good expression of everyone going their own way, chocked full of overshadowing truth. Alot of Disney style magic that broke the realism for no reason, and as you see throughout the film, everyone cocked up on a ship can wear on the relationships between each other when they don’t have a distraction or goal or objective.

The villain is clearly psychotic, with a god complex and the typical hypocrisy of a dictator. Claiming to want to create utopia, all while really, blaming the flaws in his own creations, for the need to create and torture more creatures. But I found the acting to be impeccable and then oddly teenage at times.

With Adam warlock coming through quite clearly at times as a traditional expectation of such characters. I wasn’t expecting will poulter to be so formidable as he was. Since will is one of those actors with a distinct face, and personality, it’s difficult to hide. The downplaying comedy of Adam warlock, seemed to almost overshadow the rest of the cast in potential to what an Adam warlock film could be when taken seriously from all sides, and will in the lead.
