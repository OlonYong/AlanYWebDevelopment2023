Johnny English Strikes Again

Image Source: Universal Pictures All-access

PG-13

2018

My review

This is the type of movie you can watch one time but then another and then another without being uninterested, the jokes in this movie were pure gold and some in my opinion would even offend this generation which I find hilarious. The acting was also very good. Even though some of the scenes had a very very very small hint of sky-fall like the way Johnny English's Aston Martin was revealed but that is not a bad thing, both of these movies are very good and JE - Strikes again was an acceptation. I do not understand why people are half and half about this movie, it is criminally underrated.

It's very entertaining. Good old comedy, family friendly, kids friendly. I liked the second part of the franchise more as compared to this one, however this might be the bias towards the general story line awareness maybe!
