Mission: Impossible - Dead Reckoning Part One

Image Source: IMDB

15+

2023

My review

I thought it was a good film and well made, with story that is exciting and intense, with spectacular visuals to match.

The ending is not what I expected, I thought it was going to be a cliffhanger along similar lines to "Avengers Assemble: Infinity War", instead it is a self-contained story with a slightly unreconciled conclusion- this does not subtract from the experience but it does make it a bit puzzling as to why the title has an affix of "part one'. The last three films have all been ongoing story / plot with a returning villain, yet no affixed sub-title.

The million-dollar stunt sequence involving the motorbike jump of the cliff should not have been released in the trailer and Tom Cruise - if he were to ever read my review might want to hold of what he reveals in the "part two" trailer if there is something anywhere near as breathtaking, because anything like that should come as a surprise to the audience.

If Tom Cruise is no longer going to helm the franchise after "part two" the only way that film could conclude is either with the death of Ethan Hunt or Ethan Hunt goes missing presumed dead- whilst this was not what I would have expected in "part one" of Dead Reckoning it will be ironic if that ending plays out in 'part two', since not many people would want to see an MI: Legacy movie (like "The Bourne Legacy") as it flopped badly at box office.

If you are in two minds about going to see this film, rest assured everything that you expect from a decent MI film is here, just donâ€™t expect too much of the cliffhanger at the end.
