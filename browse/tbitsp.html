The Boy in the Striped Pajamas

Image Source: The Companion

15+

2008

My review

To be honest, I read the book in school as I prefer among for all people to read it. The thing that lacked out for me was the fact as such an amazing book like the boy in striped pyjamas compared to the movie. The movie over all got confused with the timelines(chapters) as in the book. By this I mean that they would do a scene from chapter 8 but then skips some scenes from the book and goes to like chapter 13 for an example.

Another thing is that Bruno is the main character but for me Bruno, Gretel and Father didn't fit the movie unlike the book. Mother, Lieutenant Kotler and Shmuel played their roles well and actually brought the attention of them especially Mother who acted the best of all.

Watching this movie was pretty decent originally going to give it a 3 star, but when I saw that ending it was the real showstopper and caught me even. I recommend for people to read the book before watching the movie as the book gives more information about Bruno with Shmuel and then watch the movie with such a phenomenal ending as that is the best movie with such a shocking ending that I've seen in my like. Good Job Boy in Striped pyjama Movie, Good Job.
