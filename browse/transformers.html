Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

Image Source: Sanook

PG-13

2023

My review

Pretty much on par with Bumblebee and much like that film, this one reboot Transformers into a new era that has much potential.

Good characters, performances, and writing. Like other TF films there are human characters, but only Bumblebee and this one is written well enough that you like them and even better that they serve a key role in the story.

There’s so much nostalgia on display, too. G1 and BW fans will have more than a few moments of recognition. Some great thematic moments and moral points as well. Let’s not forget it’s action entertainment, too; The effects are top notch, and the action is possibly filmed the best of the whole series. It’s clear and creative in what’s going on.

It’s no surprise that the two films not led by Michael Bay in a creative and directorial role are best by far. If only it could’ve started this way.

The news that the team wants to bring more nostalgia with G1 and Beast Wars plots and characters - including a more faithful Megatron - is extremely welcome.
